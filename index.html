Loan Process
Agreement and Setup
We finalize the loan terms, set up the necessary legal and structural components, and prepare your asset by converting it into digital tokens, securing ownership in a vault
Funding and Liquidity Transfer
We issue loan tokens, sell them to liquidity providers, and transfer the collected funds directly to your wallet
Interest Payments and Compliance
You make regular interest payments according to a set schedule, with mechanisms in place to handle any payment issues, including a process for asset liquidation if necessary
Final Repayment and Asset Return
Upon full repayment of the loan, your ownership tokens are released from the vault and returned to your wallet, restoring your control over the asset
